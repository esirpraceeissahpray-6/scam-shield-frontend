How Phishing Scams Work

Phishing scams trick users into entering passwords or financial information on fake websites. Always check URLs before logging in.

Fake Job Offer Scams

Scammers promise jobs but request payment before employment. Legitimate companies never charge hiring fees.

WhatsApp Impersonation Scams

Scammers pretend to be relatives or officials and request urgent money. Always verify identity before sending funds.
