Learn About Scams

Understand both digital and physical scams to protect yourself and others.

ATM Skimming Scams

Devices placed on ATMs steal card details when you insert your card. Always check the card slot before use.

Fake Police Scams

Scammers pretend to be law enforcement and demand money or fines. Always verify identity through official channels.

Door-to-Door Fraud

Fraudsters pose as service workers or charity agents. Never give money without proper verification.

Fake Charity Scams

Scammers exploit emergencies to request donations. Always confirm legitimacy before donating.
